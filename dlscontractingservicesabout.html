About DLS Contracting Services


Since May of 2025, DLS Contracting Services has displayed excellence, commitment, and teamwork, to deliver high quality construction services to customers in the Austin area, and in Temple, TX.

We value our customers with high respect and graditude, that in which they can count on DLS Contracting Services to fulfill their remodeling needs. We appreciate all of our customers for trusting us to get the job done.

Meet the Owner!



Derek Smith


Derek is the founder and owner of DLS Contracting Services. After graduating high school, Derek began pursuing hands-on work that let him combine skill, precision, and pride in craftsmanship. In May 2025, he launched DLS Contracting Services to provide dependable remodeling and construction work to homeowners and businesses across Central Texas.

Derek started DLS Contracting Services with one goal - to give homeowners a contractor they can trust. A proud Central Texan, Derek brings years of hands-on-experience and a strong work ethic to every job site.
